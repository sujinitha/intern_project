Technology Stack

Our AI X-ray diagnosis system integrates cutting-edge Artificial Intelligence, Deep Learning, and Cloud Computing to deliver precise medical imaging analysis. By leveraging advanced Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs), Recurrent Neural Networks (RNNs), and LSTMs, our system ensures high accuracy in detecting abnormalities from X-ray images. Our backend is powered by Flask and FastAPI for efficient API handling, while a scalable cloud infrastructure on AWS ensures real-time processing and secure data storage. We use optimized image processing with OpenCV and TensorFlow to enhance medical images before analysis, improving overall diagnostic accuracy.

🔍 AI & Deep Learning Evolution

📌 Step 1: CNN Model Training

We train convolutional neural networks (CNNs) on medical X-ray datasets.

📌 Step 2: Feature Extraction with RNN

Using RNN for feature extraction, allowing AI to understand medical patterns.

📌 Step 3: LSTM for Image Captioning

AI generates human-like medical reports using LSTM for automated diagnosis interpretation.
